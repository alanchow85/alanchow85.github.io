Combining Business Process Re-engineering and Data Analytics

With the rise of Big data, a data-driven approach to business is transforming enterprises. Firms today are thinking about using data in myriad new ways to drive business values; from enhancing productivity by reducing business processing time. In order to do so with organisation data, we need to harvest the power of Data analytics and to combine with business process re-engineering to offer innovative services to the public or customers. One the other hand, business process also need to be re-engineered for data analytics.

This webpage also has some examples of web API calls, illustration of machine learning, and use of data visualisation tools like Tableau and Python.
